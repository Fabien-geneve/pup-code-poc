You are on the home page of the site PUP Code Initiative



PUP is an acronym. It means "Protect U Privacy", is of English, kind of SMS style.

The initiative has for objective to provide a data sharing solution, which will be longer stored on the servers of Google Facebook Twitter and more ...

Today I am alone to drive this initiative. This is not a satisfactory approach from my point of view, because I believe that the privacy protection of our lives affects all of us.

So please, join me!

  • The system will operate based agents installed on target devices (smartphone, tablet, personal computer).

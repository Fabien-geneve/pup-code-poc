The goal of this page is to give you some tips, to preserve your privacy

Private navigation

All the actual browsers, give a private navigation mode. I think it's the first thing to do. In this mode, the browser does not include cookies, and so preserve your anonymity .But this mode have some disadvantages: when you go on some site like Amazon, eBay, and so more other, those sites cant remember you. So you will lose the one click connection, and you need to give your name and password each time you run a visit on (depends on browser)!
Anyway, the site you are visiting and your internet provider have these informations, so it could be an half solution ! In fact, the private mode of browsers, is private at startup. When you log in into a site or an application, you are not anonymous anymore !
